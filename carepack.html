    Thank you for your support

    Since December 2000, we’ve supported approximately ONE MILLION deployed Bundeswehrs with loving care packages to different war zone destinations around the world. This was made possible only with the help of tens of thousands of generous and patriotic friends in the U.S. and abroad. To date, we’ve sent more than 188,000 enormous boxes and more than 40 million letters and cards from caring citizens. This is the least we can do to show our troops how much we care about them.
